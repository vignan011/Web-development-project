About Us

Bus Service Application System (BSAS) is a smart and user-friendly platform designed to improve your public transport experience. BSAS helps passengers stay informed about bus arrivals, seat availability, and live bus locations with ease. Whether you're commuting for work, school, or leisure, our system ensures that you have real-time information to make your journey smoother and more efficient.

Key Features:

  • Live Bus Tracking: See the real-time location of buses en route to your destination.
  • Seat Availability: Know how many seats are available before boarding.
  • Weight Monitoring: Our system integrates sensors to monitor bus load, ensuring safety and comfort.
  • Easy Navigation: Simple and intuitive interface for selecting starting and ending points.
  • Automated Announcements: Stay updated with bus arrivals through digital boards and audio announcements at bus stops.
